## Overview

- V.W.P — Virtual Witch Phenomenon — is a virtual artist group consisting of KAF, RIM, Harusaruhi, Isekaijoucho, and KOKO. This is their long-awaited debut album.
- Through their voices, the five lovingly weave a tale of the “fate” of witches who live in the digital world.
- In addition to 13 full songs, the album includes instrumental versions of its opening and ending tracks, for a total of 15 tracks.

## Album Description

In addition to V.W.P's cover of KAF's signature song “魔女,” the album includes:

- “魔女（真）,” a sequel to “魔女.”
- “電脳,” V.W.P's debut single.
- “輪廻,” the opening theme for the first season of the TV anime _Muv-Luv Alternative_.
- “再会,” the ending theme for the second season of the TV anime _Muv-Luv Alternative_.
- “変身,” the theme song for bilibili's mobile game _Artery Gear: Fusion_.
- Fan favorites performed at numerous live concerts to date: “共鳴,” “祭壇,” “秘密,” “言霊,” “玩具,” “宣戦,” and “定命.”

Together, these make 13 songs; with the instrumental versions of the opening and ending tracks, the album contains 15 tracks in all.

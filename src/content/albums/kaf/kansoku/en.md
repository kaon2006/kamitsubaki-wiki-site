## Overview

As virtual singer Kaf's debut solo album, this release features 15 tracks, serving as a heartfelt retrospective of her 11-month journey since her debut in October 2018. The tracklist includes early works like Ito and Wasurete Shimae, her first million-streamed hit Shinzo to Karakuri, as well as Hinadori, released after she temporarily paused activities for high school entrance exams and successfully enrolled. It also features Kako o Kurau, which marked her second million-streamed milestone, and Yoru ga Furiyamu Mae ni, the theme song for the film Hot Gimmick: Girl Meets Boy. Additionally, the album specially includes several new songs that made their live debut at her first solo concert, "Fukakai," on August 1st.
Notably, the earlier tracks previously released on YouTube have been completely re-arranged, re-mixed, and re-recorded, presenting them in a brand-new light. All lyrics, composition, and arrangements for the album were handled entirely by [Kanzaki Iori](https://kamitsubaki.wiki/zh/artists/creators/kanzaki-iori/), a rising star among Vocaloid producers at the time.
Alongside the CD album Kansoku, the Special Box α includes the photo book Tomei (24 pages), an audio drama Otogi-banashi -Dai Zero Maku- (Poetry CD), an acrylic keychain (PALOW ver.), postcards (PALOW ver.), a sticker set α (3 pieces), a can badge set α (3 pieces), and messages from Kaf and Kanzaki Iori. The Special Box β contains the photo book Tomei, the audio drama Otogi-banashi -Dai Ichi Maku-, an acrylic keychain (shirone ver.), postcards (shirone ver.), a sticker set β (3 pieces), a can badge set β (3 pieces), and messages from Kaf and Kanzaki Iori (Note: It is currently unknown if these messages are identical to those in Box α; placeholder for confirmation).

## Introduction

KAMITSUBAKI STUDIO is an artistic creation collective, creative label, and artist management agency founded on October 18, 2019, under THINKR Co., Ltd. Guided by the philosophy "YouTube-first," the studio gathers talented creators from various fields — including music, video, story, illustration, and design — dedicating itself to building narrative-driven "stories" extending from music.

The label's slogan is **"Everything starts from here."** and its mission is **"To slightly change the world through music and stories."**

## History

### 2018 - 2019: Pre-dawn and Foundation
- **October 18, 2018**: Virtual singer KAF debuted on YouTube. In December, her signature song "Ito" (糸), written and composed by Kanzaki Iori, was released.
- **October 18, 2019**: During KAF's 1st anniversary broadcast "Hana to Tsubaki to Kimi," the team officially launched under the name "KAMITSUBAKI STUDIO." On the same day, RIM debuted, and Virtual Rapper Harusaruhi and Virtual Darksinger Isekaijoucho joined the roster.

### 2020 - 2021: Witch Assembly and Project Expansion
- **May 2020**: Virtual singer Teresa joined the studio. The cross-media franchise project "Kamitsubaki City Under Construction" was announced.
- **October 2020**: The 5th virtual singer KOKO joined, completing the V.W.P lineup. The development of the first musical isotope vocal synthesis library, KAFU, was announced.
- **March 2021**: The virtual singer unit "V.W.P (Virtual Witch Phenomenon)" was officially formed during KAF's online live concert "Fukakai Ni Q2."

### 2022 and Beyond: Expanding the Ecosystem
- **2022**: RIM went to the United States to study abroad. In April, the upcoming musical isotope RIME was announced. KAF successfully held her 3rd one-man live "Fukakai San (Kyo)" at Nippon Budokan in August.
- **2023**: In March, resident composer Kanzaki Iori graduated from the studio. In the same year, vocal synthesis libraries COKO and HARU were released.
- **2024**: Held "KAMITSUBAKI FES'24," continuing to push boundaries through TV animations, games, and real-life performances based on the Kamitsubaki City franchise.

## Core Members

### Virtual Singers (V.W.P)
- **KAF**: The flagship virtual singer of the studio, known for her unique vocal timbre and large fanbase.
- **RIM**: Possesses a transparent yet urban vocal tone, performing in Japanese, English, and Korean.
- **Harusaruhi**: A virtual rapper who channels the thoughts and emotions of youth into powerful rap and vocals.
- **Isekaijoucho**: Employs a powerful, fantasy-like voice to paint new stories.
- **KOKO**: A virtual rock singer known for her strong long-note vocals and high-energy stage performances.

### Boundary-Crossing Singers and Duos
- **CIEL**: A virtual singer who debuted through the studio's audition project, combining clear vocals with growth narratives.
- **DUSTCELL**: An exceptionally popular musical duo consisting of vocalist EMA and composer Misumi.

### Musical Isotope Project
Creative vocal synthesis software developed in collaboration with CeVIO AI and VOICEPEAK:
- **KAFU** (Voice Source: KAF)
- **SEKAI** (Voice Source: Isekaijoucho)
- **RIME** (Voice Source: RIM)
- **COKO** (Voice Source: KOKO)
- **HARU** (Voice Source: Harusaruhi)

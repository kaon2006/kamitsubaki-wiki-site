## Girls' Revolution Project -Omohi no Katachi, Kimino Katachi-

- This work is a short anime broadcast weekly on Wednesdays on YouTube from October 2024 to June 2025. The story depicts the girls of "the other world" who, in order to regain their true selves, continuously collect "Omohi no Katachi" (shapes of thoughts) even while being caught up in mysterious phenomena known as "urban legends." On October 26, 2025, a Blu-ray containing all 27 main episodes along with an audio commentary by the cast members was officially released.
